Our Journey

Founded in 2013, South Dibrugarh Karate Do Academy has grown from a small group of enthusiastic students into a thriving community dedicated to the art of karate. Our commitment to excellence and traditional values has attracted individuals from all walks of life.

Message from President

Welcome to SDKA, where we foster discipline, respect, and excellence in martial arts with modern training methods. My vision for the South Dibrugarh Karate Do Academy is to see it thrive and succeed through the dedicated efforts of our skilled instructors. By providing proper training and guidance, we aim to ensure the continued success and growth of our club. Our goal is always to strive for the good of the SDK Academy, fostering an environment where our members can excel and achieve greatness. Together, we will build a legacy of excellence and pride for the academy.

Message from Founder

In 2013, I founded the South Dibrugarh Karate Do Academy with a vision to create a community dedicated to the traditional art of karate. Over the past ten years, I have had the honor of serving this club and witnessing its growth and achievements. My unwavering commitment is to continue striving for the improvement of our academy, ensuring we bring out the best in every member. With our dedicated team of instructors and the relentless spirit of our students, we will elevate the standards of our club and create a legacy of excellence and pride for the SDK Academy.

Mr.Naba Sharma
